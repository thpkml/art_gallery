A bit of our history

Acton Art Gallery known more commonly among the locals as AAG was estabhlished in 1932 by the local council. The primary purpose of the estabhishment was to encourage the aspiring artists into all sorts of arts as well as to teach the general population to actively appreciate art.



Arts and Artists

At present the humble council gallery has transformed into one of the globally recognized prestigious British gallery. It houses works of several renowned artists of the past and the present. We have been able enrich our collection with the works of Salvador Dali, Pablo Picasso, Van Gogh among others. Works of popular modern artists such as the graffiti king 'Banksy' and the great Korean sketching master Kim Jung-gi regularly make appearance at AAG.



Where we are

Our precious arts live in this beautiful vitorian building at the northern corner of Acton. The famous building was renovated for the purpose in 1968 and is constantly refurbished to accomodate all modern conviniences like heating, air conditioning etc. We also have a lovely in-house cafe that serves healthy wholefood items at a reasonable price if you feel bit peckish after a tour of the gallery.
