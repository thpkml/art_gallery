Our Celebrated Artists


Kim Jung-ji

"Dragon Fighter"
"War Hogs"
"Submarines"
"Live Drawing"

Kim Jung Ji is an artist from South Korea who has recently made his name in the world's Sketching artists' hall of fame. His unique way of using mostly pen brushes and detailing extreme minutes in the drawings is quite hypnotising.

Banksy

"Come back..!"
"Flower Power"
"What we do in .."
"Rent due"

'Banksy' is an alias for the mysterious English graffiti artist whom people have claimed to see/identify in multiple occasions without much evidence. One thing we know for sure about Banksy is that his art has revolutionised graffiti/mural from vandalism to revered collection arts.

Salvador Dali

"The Persistence - 1931"
"Elephants - 1944"
"Metamorphosis of Narcisus - 1937"
"Swans Reflecting.. - 1937"

Dali, full name Salvador Felipe Jacinto Dali was born in Figures, Spain in 1904. He got to work together with contemporary artists like Pablo Picasso whose works might have propelled him towards surrealism. He became so popular with his dreamlike surrealism of his paintings, we now consider him one of the greatest painters that ever lived !

Pablo Picasso

"Picasso - 1"
"Picasso - 2"
"Picasso - 3"
"Picasso - 4"

Well Picasso does not need any introduction if you are even slightly interested in arts, especially the genre of 'Cubism' made popular worldwide by his famous works. Visit AAG to get astounded by some of his mind-bending Cubism paintings.

Vincent VanGogh

"Van Gogh - 1"
"Van Gogh - 2"
"Van Gogh - 3"
"Van Gogh - 4"

Vincent Van Gogh is the greatest Dutch painter after Rembrandt. However, despite producing such amazing pieces of artwork and trail-blazing a completely new style of art, he remained relatively unknown and quite poor during his lifetime. Visit AAG to marvel at his fancy brushwork and pay respect to this giant of an artist that he deserves !
