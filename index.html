This summer June - September 2018, Acton Art Gallery is hosting local artists from acton and around in one of a kind art display and competition. There will be surprises, from what we have been able to collect so far with more impressive works to arrive. It has been a fascinating journey and a revealing experience for us involved in the project seeing so much potential in the Acton artists, young and old. Please visit the times and tickets page to find out more on the event. If you have any queries, feel free to leave us a a message on contact page.
Please Note:The gallery will also continue to open as usual with all our famous artists' works during the event.

Acton Art Gallery known more commonly among the locals as AAG was estabhlished in 1932 by the local council. The primary purpose of the estabhishment was to encourage the aspiring artists into all sorts of arts as well as to teach the general population to actively appreciate art.

At present the humble council gallery has transformed into one of the globally recognized prestigious British gallery. It houses works of several renowned artists of the past and the present. We have been able enrich our collection with the works of Salvador Dali, Pablo Picasso, Van Gogh read more..

Artists whose works are currently on display

1. Salvador Dali

Dali's works have been at AAG since the early 70's and it started with the donation of the 'piece1' from the National Art Gallery. We are proud to be displaying five of his prestigious works at our gallery.

2. Pablo Picasso

Well Picasso does not need any introduction if you are even slightly interested in arts, especially the genre of 'Cubism' made popular worldwide by his famous works. Visit AAG to get astounded by some of his mind-bending Cubism paintings.

3. Kim Jung Ji

Kim Jung Ji is an artist from South Korea who has recently made his name in the world's Sketching artists' hall of fame. His unique way of using mostly pen brushes and detailing extreme minutes in the drawings is quite hypnotising.
